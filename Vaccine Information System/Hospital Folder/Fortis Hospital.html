About:


Fortis Escorts Heart Institute is one of the best cardiac care center in Delhi/NCR. This institute is well known for using latest and advanced technology in cardiac bypass surgery, non invasive cardiology and paediatric cardiac surgery to name a few. It has an advanced set of laboratories for nuclear medicines, heamtology, radiology, biochemistry, microbiology and transfusion medicine.


Fortis Hospital, New Delhi with regard to the COVID-19 situation, has continued to successfully treat patients and address their health needs both in person & through virtual consults, strictly adhering to the regulations advised by the corporation and following international infection control practises.
